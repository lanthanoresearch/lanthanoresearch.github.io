About Lanthano Research

Who Are We?

That is not revealed.

Our identities remain hidden. The focus is not on personalities, credentials, or public recognition. The focus is on the ideas, evidence, and arguments presented.

What Do We Do?

We investigate, document, and analyze theories, historical claims, biblical topics, unexplained events, and controversial ideas.

What began as private research and the writing of in-depth papers has grown into a public effort to share findings, raise questions, and challenge accepted narratives.

Our goal is not to demand belief, but to encourage examination.

Every claim should be tested. Every assumption should be questioned. Every idea should stand or fall on the strength of its evidence.

Our Purpose

Lanthano exists to explore what others overlook.
